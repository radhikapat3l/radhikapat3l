## How I Think

This is the part most profiles skip.

- **I prioritize by impact × adoption, not by what's loudest in the room** — a tool nobody uses is a feature that didn't ship
- **I think in funnels, not features** — every drop-off is a product problem, not a user problem
- **I measure success by behavior change, not launch metrics** — CSAT, adoption rate, and cycle time reduction over vanity numbers
- **I work backwards from the outcome** — what does "done" actually look like for the business, and what's the fastest path there
- **I respect operational complexity** — regulated environments, institutional clients, and cross-functional teams are constraints I've worked inside, not around
- **I ship, then iterate** — a good product in production beats a perfect product in planning
